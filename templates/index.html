ABOUT US

ESYA

Tracing The Distant

ESYA, IIIT Delhi’s annual tech fest is the epitome of celebrating all things tech and beyond. Celebrating the coder, the reader, the learner, the leader. ESYA provides them a platform to showcase their ideas and build upon them; to morph their unrelenting imagination into a reality. After 8 successful editions Esya is back to challenge minds and transcend the norm. The nouveau 9th edition of Esya comes power packed with a tantalising fusion of never before seen events along with the classic headliners.

This two day technical fest of IIIT Delhi showcases a plethora of events ranging from coding competitions to overnight hackathons, from powerful debates to robots at war. The new edition of ESYA, “Techfusion - Tracing the Distant” is bigger, bolder and better than ever before. Come join us in our pursuit of infinity and beyond.
